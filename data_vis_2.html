Australian Agricultural Output 2020-21

Australia's agricultural industry is diverse and critical to both its economy and food supply. This visualization explores the agricultural output by state, providing insights into the distribution and value of various commodities. The choropleth map below illustrates the gross value of agricultural output across different states in Australia. By using a brown-to-green gradient, the map highlights regions with lower values in brown and those with higher values in green, offering an immediate visual contrast.
